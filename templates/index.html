Here are some of my projects.

mystic encryption

A flask application that encrypts any message you put into it with a unique key that is generated when you create your account. It uses SQL to create queries that allow you to register an account, login, and obtain your encryption key.

mystic inventory manager

A flask application that stores inventory data for any product you would want to keep track of. Each account gets one inventory table created when you register. it uses sql queries to pull information from the table and also store new information that is input by the user.

Mystic Stock Checker

A work in progress stock checker that will check the stock of graphics cards on the best buy website. When it discovers that a card is in stock it will send a notification to a discord server. It also has the capability to send an email notification to an email list which you can add yourself to once you are in the server.

My Story

Ever since I was a kids I have always loved computers and wanted to do things involving them. I loved building them and tinkering with them. At the age of 12 I built my first computer made out of scrap parts that my dad garbage picked. This computer was my pride and joy! Until I knocked it off the table and the hard drive, which was not secured, fell and corrupted all of the data on it... R.I.P First Aid merit badge book. Once I got my first job at 16 I saved up money to build my first computer. It had a GTX 1070 graphics card with an i5-6600k CPU and was my first real step into the world of computers. With this computer I could do lots of things that my other machines were too slow to do and then began my journey into programming. It first started out using simple Auto Hotkey scripts to click on cookie clicker and then ended up with me making many programs and websites that would use technologies a lot more powerful and versatile than Auto Hotkey was. We are here today and I am glad to say that my journey has barely started. I have a lot more to learn and I am excited for what the future has in store for me.
